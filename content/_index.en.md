Located in the city of Burgos, at Quintana Abogados, we have over 40 years of experience in legal advice in Spain, as well as in defending our clients' interests across [different areas of law](servicios) with professionalism, efficiency, and ethical commitment. We are constantly updating our knowledge and striving for excellence, with absolute respect for the rules governing the practice of law, in particular:

* Loyalty to clients.  
* Independence and freedom in ensuring defense and client advice.  
* Respect for professional secrecy and the confidentiality of entrusted matters.  
* Professional competence.  
* Prevention of conflicts of interest.  
* Integrity in matters of professional fees.

> Honesty, integrity, rectitude, loyalty, diligence, and truthfulness are virtues that must guide every action. They are the foundation of the necessary trust-based relationships with clients and form the basis of the honor and dignity of the profession. One must always act honestly and diligently, with competence, loyalty to the client, respect for the opposing party, and must preserve confidentiality regarding everything learned through the practice of the profession.

(Spanish Code of Legal Ethics)
